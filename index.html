Why 'GTM QuickShare'?

We Synchronize Your Team!

GTM QuickShare is an internal file repository built to house your teams Excel Macros, Workbooks, user guides, documentation, and any piece of software that needs constant attention. The days of repetitively sending group emails to your colleagues about minor updates to your tax software are over. The QuickShare repository makes accessing files as easy as clicking a download link. We keep all of your files categorized and organized, synchronously!

It's Stupid Easy.

GTM QuickShare is a light weight repository to upload and receive tax software and documents. We have no account systems or needless add-ons and services that extend or complicate our core functionality. We provide an easily readable interface so you can get what you need, quick and simple!

Are You Sick of Seeing This?

We know how important it is that your automation tools run as quick and efficiently as possible. We check, backup, and test our files daily to ensure the chances of annoying runtime errors or outdated workbooks happen less. This way, you can spend less time inquiring about broken Macros and more time using them.

QuickShare Feeds Your Laziness!

We can't stress enough that GTM QuickShare is stupid easy to use. Got a file you want to upload? A simple shortcut will bring up the file explorer, and our smart autofill will get the details of your file so you don't have to (unless you want to). You have complete control of the files you upload. You can edit, delete, or even change the storage location in which your file is being hosted. We know you just want a fast, reliable source to store and share your files, and GTM QuickShare is that solution.
